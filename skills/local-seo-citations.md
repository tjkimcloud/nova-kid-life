# Skill: Local SEO — Citations

**Activation:** Any session involving launch setup, off-site presence building, or citation audits.

---

## What Citations Are

Citations = any online mention of your business name, even without a link. They validate that your business is real and where you say it is. The key factor is **NAP consistency** — identical Name, Address, Phone across all listings.

---

## Citation Hierarchy — Submit in Order

These 4 sites feed data to most other directories automatically. Submit all 4 before doing any Tier 2 work. Inconsistencies here cascade everywhere.

## NAP Consistency Rules — CRITICAL

Every citation must use EXACTLY this format. No variations, ever.

```
Name:    NovaKidLife                     ← never "Nova Kid Life" or "NoVa Kid Life"
Address: Northern Virginia, VA           ← or omit address (service area business)
Phone:   (omit if not using one)
Website: https://novakidlife.com         ← always https, no trailing slash
```

Audit citations quarterly. Use BrightLocal or Moz Local to find inconsistencies.

## Common Local SEO Mistakes — Avoid These

| Mistake | Why it hurts | Fix |
|---------|-------------|-----|
| Inconsistent NAP | Confuses Google about which business is real | Audit all citations, fix mismatches |
| Keyword stuffing in GBP name | Violates GBP policy, can get listing suspended | Use exact business name only |
| Buying reviews | Google algorithmic penalty + policy violation | Build reviews organically |
| Thin location pages | Duplicate content penalty | Add unique 200+ word intro per city page |
| Ignoring GBP Q&A | Competitors or bad actors can post misinformation | Seed + monitor Q&A (see local-seo-gbp.md) |
| City in meta every time but not in content | Looks manipulative | Integrate geography naturally into copy |
| Not responding to negative reviews | Trust signal drops | Respond within 24h, stay professional |
| Duplicate GBP listings | Splits authority, confuses Google | Report and merge duplicates |
| Using a P.O. box as address | GBP policy violation for service area businesses | Use service area only, no address |
| Ignoring Bing | ChatGPT + Copilot use Bing for local | Bing Places setup is 20 minutes — do it |
