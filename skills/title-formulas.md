## Title Tag Character Rules (from seo-technical.md)

- Hard limit: 60 characters (Google truncates at ~60)
- Minimum: 40 characters (too short = missed keyword opportunity)
- Always include a Virginia/NoVa geographic signal
- Use em-dash `—` to separate event name from city
- Use pipe `|` before brand name `NovaKidLife`
- Count all punctuation characters

---

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

- Do NOT duplicate the event title word-for-word in H1 AND title tag — they can vary slightly
- Do NOT omit the geographic signal — every page must have VA, Virginia, or NoVa
- Do NOT exceed 60 chars — truncated titles lose CTR
- Do NOT use clickbait ("You won't believe...") — parent audience values clarity over curiosity gaps
- Do NOT use ALL CAPS — reads as shouting, penalized in some search contexts
