## Instagram Hashtag Playbook (10–15 per post)

Mix these three groups on every NoVa post:

**Local (always use — 3–4 tags):**
`#NorthernVirginia #NoVaKids #FairfaxVA #LoudounCounty #ArlingtonVA #RestonVA`

**Topic (match to event type — 4–6 tags):**
`#KidsActivities #FamilyFun #FreeKidsEvents #StorytimeKids #STEMKids #PokemonTCG #PokemonCards`

**Seasonal (add when relevant — 2–3 tags):**
`#FallFun #SummerKids #HolidayEvents #PumpkinPatch`

**Strategy:** 3–4 local + 4–6 topic + 2–3 seasonal = 10–13 tags. Never exceed 15 — diminishing returns.

---

## Twitter Hashtag Rules

- Maximum 3 hashtags per tweet (4 absolute max — more suppresses reach)
- Always include 1 local tag (`#NoVaKids`, `#FairfaxVA`, etc.)
- Always include 1 topic tag (`#FreeKidsEvents`, `#PokemonTCG`, etc.)
- Optional 3rd: seasonal or city-specific
- Place hashtags at the end of the post, never inline

---

## Facebook Hashtag Rules

- 1–2 hashtags maximum on Facebook posts
- Facebook hashtag reach is minimal — use for discoverability only, not as a strategy
- Recommended: `#NoVaKids` + one event-type tag
